Website Plan: Explore South Africa 🇿🇦

Site Name

Explore South Africa

This name was chosen because it clearly represents a travel-focused website that highlights the beauty, culture, and attractions of South Africa.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information about travel destinations in South Africa. It will showcase popular attractions, cultural experiences, and travel tips to help users explore and plan their trips.

Scenarios

  • What are the best places to visit in South Africa?
  • What activities can I do while traveling in South Africa?

Color Schema

Primary Color (#007a4d) – Used for headings and navigation

Secondary Color (#ffb612) – Used for accents and highlights

Typography

Playfair Display – Used for headings

Roboto – Used for body text
